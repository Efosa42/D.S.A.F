Who We Are

Delta State Association was formed in 2013 and registered at the patent office in Finland. It is located in the greater Helsinki region of Finland. The association is non political and non profit. Our aims include bringing together people of Delta State origin that are domiciled in Finland, in order to strengthen our social-economic and cultural heritage. DSF already put in place an effective support system for all members. These are among other efforts to further promote proper integration and welfare packages for members.

What We Do

The association organizes interactive sections on a regular basis to boost our social-cultural activities, at the same time encourage and maintain regular contact with members. We arrange several events annually to actualize our core objectives and goals. DSF engages and partakes in several projects that create impact on our members. The association also cooperates and partner with other organizations including the Nigerian embassy.


Projects

It is our vision to identify and engage in collaboration projects to assist less privileged children of school age both in Nigeria and Finland. DSF is engaged in several family programs/events to promote social interaction and is mainly targeted at children/youths.
