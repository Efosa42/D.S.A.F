Who We Are

Delta State Association was formed in 2013 and registered at the patent office in Finland. It is located in the greater Helsinki region of Finland. The association is non political and non profit. Our aims include bringing together people of Delta State origin that are domiciled in Finland, in order to strengthen our social-economic and cultural heritage. DSAF already put in place an effective support system for all members. These are among other efforts to further promote proper integration and welfare packages for members.

What We Do


The association organizes interactive sections on a regular basis to boost our social-cultural activities, at the same time encourage and maintain regular contact with members. We arrange several events annually to actualize our core objectives and goals. DSAF engages and partakes in several projects that create impact on our members. The association also cooperates and partner with other organizations including the Nigerian embassy.
